## 调整范围

这一轮调整会先集中在官网的基础体验：导航结构、信息流、账户入口和部分页面的暗色模式会被统一到新的视觉系统中。

当前页面仍处在 Beta 阶段。我们会保留可用功能，同时逐步替换旧页面中的临时样式、硬编码文本和不一致的交互状态。

- 首页信息流会从 Markdown 内容中读取
- 正文页会保留加载骨架
- 后续可以继续补充配图、日期和跳转路径

## Scope

This round of changes focuses first on the core website experience: navigation, feed layout, account entry points and dark mode coverage are being aligned with the refreshed visual system.

The current pages are still in beta. Existing usable features will remain available while temporary styles, hard-coded text and inconsistent interaction states are replaced over time.

- The home feed reads from Markdown content
- Article pages keep a loading skeleton
- Images, dates and links can be updated later

The feed will be used for website progress, server service notes and feature updates. Later entries will include fuller article bodies, images and destination links.
