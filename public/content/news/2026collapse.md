## 《陷落》：Doc. Steinbeck 博士的城市实验

三座城市，十五枚密钥，至多 144 小时的围城之战。

这一切都出自 Doc. Steinbeck 博士之手。

现在，这位疯狂的博士接管了广播，将三座城市和所有参赛者一同变成了他的长期实验样本。

当防线被点亮，当指南针开始转动，每一次出征都可能改写整座城市的命运。《陷落》是一场持续数天的阵营生存对抗活动：玩家将加入 A（旧主城与芙岛）、B（主城）或 C（锡城）阵营，在熟悉的城区中筑起堡垒、刺探敌情、争夺密钥，并通过战斗、资源采集与团队协作为自己的城市赢得生机——而博士会在某个看不见的地方，饶有兴致地记录这一切。

守住自己的密钥，夺走敌人的希望。若 144 小时内只剩一个阵营存活，它将立即加冕；若多座城市坚持到最后，则由积分、密钥与战绩决定谁有资格见证黎明。

“请不必紧张，各位受试者。你们只需要证明一件事：当世界开始陷落时，谁最值得活到最后？”——Doc. Steinbeck 博士

## 选择你的城市

A 阵营主要活动于出生点周边、旧主城和芙岛，主题色为棕红色。

![A 阵营：旧主城与芙岛](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/group_oldcity.png)

B 阵营主要活动于主城、全物品、老工业区及周边建筑，主题色为蓝色。

![B 阵营：主城](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/group_maincity.png)

C 阵营主要活动于锡城及其周边，主题色为绿色。

![C 阵营：锡城](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/group_hitcity.png)

每座城市都拥有自己的主场优势。选择你的阵营，也选择你的战斗方式：

- A 阵营（旧主城与芙岛）：在己方阵营区域内移动速度提高 5%。
- B 阵营（主城）：饥饿消耗降低，更不容易饥饿。
- C 阵营（锡城）：挖掘木头、泥土和石头类方块的速度提高 10%。

玩家只能在本阵营区域内自然复活。若有效床点或复活点不可用，系统会在本阵营区域内寻找安全位置复活。

## 十五枚密钥，十五条命脉

密钥是《陷落》的核心，也是每座城市仍未熄灭的证明。每个阵营开局拥有 5 个密钥：它们既维系阵营的存活，也会持续带来积分。失去最后一枚密钥，灭亡的倒计时便会开始。

放置状态的密钥是一个 `5×7×5` 的区域，区域会显示阵营颜色描边，并在中心显示竖直悬浮的钥匙粒子。玩家右键地面放置密钥时，密钥会放在被点击方块的上方。

密钥可以处于两种主要状态：

- 放置状态：固定在世界中，可以提供积分，也可以被敌方夺取。
- 物品状态：存在于玩家背包或掉落物中，可以携带、护送、放置或启动自毁。

密钥必须放在己方区域内，并且周围应当可被正常玩家接近。把密钥卡进不可进入空间、窒息空间、基岩下方或明显不可交互位置，会被管理员裁定违规。

### 刷新密钥：绝境中的第二次机会

博士声称，一次机会无法产生足够可靠的实验数据。活动进行期间，系统每 24 小时会为每个仍然存活的阵营增加 1 枚刷新密钥。

刷新密钥的有效期为 2 小时。需要特别注意：在有效期的最后 10 分钟内，它不再计入阵营的有效密钥数量。请在时间耗尽前规划好防守与下一步行动——不要让最后十分钟成为城市覆灭的倒计时。

## 开战前的两小时

活动开始后的前 2 小时是部署阶段，也是你们加固城墙、规划交通、藏好命脉的最后机会。

在部署阶段内：

- 密钥不能被定位。
- 密钥不能被夺取。
- 密钥不能被摧毁。
- 各阵营应尽快选择地点放置初始密钥。
- 可以建设防御、交通、观察点和基地设施。

部署阶段结束后，密钥正式激活。未放置的初始密钥会作为物品发放给该阵营在线成员；若无人在线，则进入阵营公共密钥池，等待成员上线领取。

## 夺取密钥：带回荣耀，或让它化为灰烬

敌方玩家站在放置状态密钥区域内并连续停留 6 秒，即可夺取该密钥。夺取过程中会显示进度条。中途离开区域会重置进度。

密钥被夺取后，原放置密钥消失，夺取者获得一个物品状态密钥，并为其阵营获得夺取积分。

夺取只是开始。接下来，你必须在两种命运中做出选择。

第一种是护送回己方区域并放置。成功后，该密钥会转化为己方密钥，己方获得较高积分，原阵营永久失去该密钥。

第二种是启动自毁。玩家手持密钥按丢弃键会进入 5 秒确认，再次丢弃才会启动 10 分钟自毁倒计时。倒计时期间密钥仍然绑定在玩家身上，玩家死亡时密钥会掉落。倒计时完成后密钥被摧毁，持有者阵营获得较少积分。

## 猎手的指南针

“猎物不知道自己已经被发现时，实验最有趣。”——Doc. Steinbeck 博士

阵营可以消耗积分购买密钥指南针。指南针会锁定指定敌方阵营的一个随机放置状态密钥，持续 20 分钟，每 30 秒刷新一次方向。

指南针只会指向放置状态密钥，不会指向物品状态密钥。每个阵营同时最多拥有 3 个有效指南针。

当指南针持有者距离目标密钥小于 20 格时，该密钥坐标会被精确揭露给持有者阵营，持续 3 分钟。被定位阵营会收到高危警报。

如果指南针锁定的密钥被夺取、转化、摧毁或作废，指南针会自毁，不会自动切换到下一个目标。

## 每一次行动都在改写战局

每个阵营共享积分。积分既是最终结算依据，也可以用于购买补给、增益和侦察工具。

主要积分来源包括：

- 对敌方玩家造成实际伤害。
- 击杀或助攻击杀敌方玩家。
- 放置状态密钥每 10 分钟提供持续积分。
- 成功夺取敌方密钥。
- 成功将敌方密钥带回并转化。
- 成功自毁敌方密钥。
- 挖掘钻石、绿宝石、红石、深层煤炭和远古残骸。
- 使用鞘翅高速飞行。

主要扣分包括：

- 阵营玩家死亡。
- 持有密钥时死亡。
- 己方密钥被夺取、自毁或转化。
- 违规放置密钥或恶意利用漏洞。

伤害得分存在短时间上限，防止对刷。

## 旧主城与芙岛的生命线

A 阵营拥有两个固定传送站，用于连接旧主城与芙岛。传送站区域为 `6×3×6`，边界使用蓝色粒子描边。站点位置由地图预设，玩家不能创建、移动或自定义传送目的地。

![芙岛传送站界面](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/tp_fuis.png)

![旧主城传送站界面](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/tp_oldcity.png)

玩家进入所属阵营传送站并连续停留 3 秒后，可以传送到连接站点。

传送站不能用于直接规避密钥护送风险。以下情况不能使用传送站：

- 处于战斗状态。
- 持有任何物品状态密钥。
- 正在执行密钥自毁倒计时。
- 刚夺取密钥且尚未成功放置或自毁该密钥。
- 距离敌方放置状态密钥过近。
- 传送站正在被干扰。

敌方玩家进入传送站区域并连续停留 8 秒，可以使传送站进入 10 分钟干扰状态。所属阵营玩家可以在传送站区域内连续停留 15 秒进行修复。

## 死亡不是终点，失去密钥才是

玩家死亡次数越多，复活等待时间越长。死亡会扣除阵营积分，持有密钥时死亡会额外扣分。

复活后会获得短暂保护。若玩家主动攻击敌人，保护会立即取消。复活保护不能用于夺取密钥。

若某阵营没有任何有效密钥，会进入 10 分钟濒危状态。若 10 分钟内没有恢复有效密钥，该阵营出局。出局后，该阵营玩家转为旁观者，剩余密钥失效，积分冻结。

## 成为最后一座不曾陷落的城市

若某阵营成为唯一存活阵营，则立即获胜。

若最长游戏时间 144 小时结束时仍有多个阵营存活，则按以下顺序判定胜负：

1. 总积分。
2. 有效密钥数量。
3. 摧毁或转化敌方密钥次数。
4. 击杀数。
5. 死亡数更少者。

若仍然无法分出胜负，则进入 30 分钟加时。加时期间所有放置密钥坐标公开，密钥持续积分停止，指南针免费。

## 出征之前

活动期间死亡不掉落关闭，游戏难度固定为困难。跳过黑夜需要所有在线玩家睡觉。

禁止外挂、矿透、X-Ray 材质包、自动瞄准、飞行作弊、复制物品、利用漏洞、恶意卡服、恶意堆积实体，以及通过断线重连规避死亡或密钥风险。

集结你的队友，选定你的城市，藏好你们的密钥。

当部署阶段结束，所有人都会听见战争开始的声音，Doc. Steinbeck 博士也会启动他的计时器。

你准备好让敌人的城市先一步陷落了吗？博士已经在观察了。

## Collapse: Dr. Steinbeck's Experiment in Urban Survival

Three cities. Fifteen keys. Up to 144 hours under siege.

Every part of it is the work of Dr. Steinbeck.

No one knows when he completed the lockdown, or where he concealed the laboratory crowded with instruments, red buttons, and frantically scribbled equations. Now the mad doctor has seized the airwaves and turned three cities—and every participant inside them—into subjects in his long-term experiment. He intends to document how loyalty breaks, how order collapses, and just how much people will sacrifice for a single key.

When the defenses light up and the compass needle begins to turn, every expedition can change the fate of an entire city. Collapse is a multi-day faction survival event. Join Faction A (Old Spawn and Fu Island), Faction B (Spawn), or Faction C (Tin City), then build fortifications, scout hostile territory, capture keys, and earn points through combat, resource gathering, and teamwork—while the doctor records everything from somewhere beyond your sight.

Protect your keys and steal the enemy's last hope. If only one faction remains before the 144-hour limit, it wins immediately. If multiple cities survive, points, keys, and combat records will decide who lives to see the dawn.

“Please, remain calm, test subjects. You need only prove one thing: when the world begins to collapse, who deserves to survive until the end?” —Dr. Steinbeck

## Choose Your City

Faction A operates around the world spawn, Old Spawn, and Fu Island. Its faction color is brown-red.

![Faction A: Old Spawn and Fu Island](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/group_oldcity.png)

Faction B controls Spawn, the All Items area, the Old Industrial District, and nearby buildings. Its faction color is blue.

![Faction B: Spawn](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/group_maincity.png)

Faction C is based in and around Tin City. Its faction color is green.

![Faction C: Tin City](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/group_hitcity.png)

Each city offers a different home-field advantage. Choose your faction—and your way of fighting:

- Faction A (Old Spawn and Fu Island): +5% movement speed inside its own territory.
- Faction B (Spawn): reduced hunger consumption.
- Faction C (Tin City): +10% mining speed for wood, dirt, and stone-type blocks.

Players can respawn naturally only inside their faction's territory. If a valid bed or respawn point is unavailable, the system will find a safe position within that territory.

## Fifteen Keys, Fifteen Lifelines

Keys are the heart of Collapse, and proof that a city is still alive. Each faction begins with 5 keys. They keep the faction in the game and continuously generate points. Lose your final key, and the countdown to elimination begins.

A deployed key occupies a `5×7×5` area. The area is outlined in the faction's color, with vertical floating key particles at its center. When a player right-clicks the ground to deploy a key, it is placed above the clicked block.

Keys have two main states:

- Deployed: fixed in the world, generating points but vulnerable to enemy capture.
- Item: carried in an inventory or dropped in the world; it can be escorted, deployed, or set to self-destruct.

Keys must be deployed within their faction's territory and remain normally accessible to players. Hiding one inside an inaccessible or suffocating space, beneath bedrock, or in any clearly uninteractable location will be ruled a violation by the administrators.

### Refresh Keys: A Second Chance at the Brink

The doctor insists that a single chance cannot produce sufficiently reliable data. Every 24 hours, the system adds 1 Refresh Key to each faction that is still alive.

A Refresh Key remains valid for 2 hours. During the final 10 minutes of that period, however, it no longer counts toward the faction's number of valid keys. Plan your defense and your next move before time runs out—do not let those final ten minutes become your city's countdown to destruction.

## The Two Hours Before War

The first 2 hours of the event are the deployment phase—your last chance to reinforce the walls, plan routes, and conceal your lifelines.

During deployment:

- Keys cannot be tracked.
- Keys cannot be captured.
- Keys cannot be destroyed.
- Each faction should deploy its starting keys as soon as possible.
- Defenses, transport routes, observation posts, and base infrastructure may be built.

When deployment ends, the keys activate. Any undeployed starting key will be issued as an item to an online member of its faction. If no member is online, it enters the faction's shared key pool until someone logs in to claim it.

## Capture a Key: Bring Home Glory, or Burn It to Ash

An enemy player can capture a deployed key by remaining inside its area for 6 uninterrupted seconds. A progress bar is displayed during the attempt; leaving the area resets all progress.

Once captured, the deployed key disappears. The captor receives it as an item, and their faction earns capture points.

Capture is only the beginning. The stolen key now faces one of two fates.

The first is to escort it into your own territory and deploy it. The key converts to your faction, your side receives the larger point reward, and its original faction loses it permanently.

The second is self-destruction. While holding the key, press the drop key once to begin a 5-second confirmation window, then press it again to start a 10-minute self-destruct countdown. The key remains bound to its carrier during the countdown and drops if the carrier dies. When the timer expires, the key is destroyed and the carrier's faction receives a smaller point reward.

## The Hunter's Compass

“The experiment is most entertaining when the prey does not yet know it has been found.” —Dr. Steinbeck

Factions can spend points on a key compass. It locks onto one random deployed key belonging to a chosen enemy faction for 20 minutes, updating its direction every 30 seconds.

The compass tracks only deployed keys, never item keys. A faction may have no more than 3 active compasses at once.

When the compass holder comes within 20 blocks of the target, the key's exact coordinates are revealed to their faction for 3 minutes. The targeted faction receives a high-risk alert.

If the tracked key is captured, converted, destroyed, or invalidated, the compass destroys itself instead of switching to another target.

## Every Action Changes the Battle

Points are shared across each faction. They determine final standings and can also be spent on supplies, buffs, and reconnaissance tools.

Main sources of points include:

- Dealing actual damage to enemy players.
- Killing or assisting in killing enemy players.
- Keeping keys deployed, which generates points every 10 minutes.
- Capturing an enemy key.
- Returning and converting an enemy key.
- Successfully self-destructing an enemy key.
- Mining diamonds, emeralds, redstone, deepslate coal, and ancient debris.
- High-speed elytra flight.

Main point penalties include:

- A faction member dying.
- Dying while carrying a key.
- A friendly key being captured, self-destructed, or converted.
- Illegal key placement or deliberate exploit abuse.

Damage scoring has a short-term cap to prevent point farming.

## The Lifeline Between Old Spawn and Fu Island

Faction A has two fixed teleport stations connecting Old Spawn and Fu Island. Each station occupies a `6×3×6` area outlined with blue particles. Their locations and destinations are fixed by the map; players cannot create, move, or customize them.

![Fu Island teleport station interface](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/tp_fuis.png)

![Old Spawn teleport station interface](https://bucket.glowingstone.cn/tp_oldcity.png)

A member of the owning faction can teleport to the linked station by remaining inside the station area for 3 uninterrupted seconds.

Teleport stations cannot be used to bypass the danger of escorting a key. A station is unavailable when a player:

- Is in combat.
- Is carrying any item-state key.
- Has an active key self-destruct countdown.
- Has captured a key but has not yet deployed or destroyed it.
- Is too close to an enemy deployed key.
- Is inside a jammed station.

An enemy can jam a station for 10 minutes by remaining inside it for 8 uninterrupted seconds. Members of the owning faction can repair it by remaining in the area for 15 uninterrupted seconds.

## Death Is Not the End—Losing Every Key Is

Respawn time increases as a player accumulates deaths. Every death costs faction points, with an additional penalty for dying while carrying a key.

Players receive brief protection after respawning. Attacking an enemy immediately removes it, and respawn protection cannot be used to capture a key.

If a faction has no valid keys, it enters a 10-minute critical state. If it does not recover a valid key before the timer expires, the faction is eliminated. Its members become spectators, its remaining keys are invalidated, and its score is frozen.

## Be the Last City Standing

If only one faction remains alive, it wins immediately.

If multiple factions remain when the 144-hour limit expires, the winner is determined in this order:

1. Total points.
2. Number of valid keys.
3. Number of enemy keys destroyed or converted.
4. Kills.
5. Fewest deaths.

If the tie still cannot be broken, the event enters a 30-minute overtime. During overtime, the coordinates of every deployed key become public, passive key scoring stops, and compasses are free.
